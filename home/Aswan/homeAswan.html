    Offer hotels
  • Tulip (formerly Helnan)
  • Located on the Nile in the city center, Helnan Aswan Hotel offers beautiful views of the Nile. It features a fully equipped gym, table tennis and various entertainment options such as bowling and cinema.
  • All rooms and chalets feature a balcony overlooking the Nile and are furnished in a homely style. All rooms offer a TV with satellite channels, a minibar and an en suite bathroom with a shower.
  • Helnan Aswan Hotel offers a wide range of buffets and a la carte menus where guests can savor their meals while enjoying the views of the Nile. Cocktails are served at the Lobby Bar, Pool Bar and Isis Bar.
  • The hotel's health club offers a sauna, steam bath and various massage treatments, all at an additional fee. There is also a children's playground at the hotel.
  • Guests can visit the Tomb of the Nobles which is a 10-minute speedboat ride from Helnan Aswan Hotel. Aswan Airport is 21 km away. Free private parking is available on site.
