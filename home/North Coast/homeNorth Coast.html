    About The Hotel
  • Porto Marina Resort
  • Overlooking the Mediterranean Sea in El Alamein, the 5-star Porto Marina resort offers luxurious accommodation, a lagoon pool and a variety of health and beauty treatments in the wellness center.
  • All rooms and suites are fitted with elegant furnishings and artwork, air conditioning, TV and an en suite bathroom. Some rooms offer beautiful lake or sea views.
  • Porto Marina Resort is set in an attractive landscaped garden and features a large indoor pool, fitness center and tennis court. There is also a playground for younger guests.
  • Porto Marina has a Mediterranean cuisine restaurant which serves buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner. There is also a beach bar serving cocktails.
  • The beaches on the northern coast of Egypt are less than one kilometer from the hotel. Alexandria is 160 km away, while Burj Al Arab and El Alamein Airport are less than 60 km away from the hotel.
